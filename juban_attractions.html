JUBAN ATTRACTIONS

Old Spanish Colonial House

These old houses are what we call bahay na bato. Their foundation is made from Spanish-style solid stone bricks where the wooden upper-story stands. These particular houses in Juban were constructed in 1898 and are known for their exquisite pre-Spanish windows and design made of capiz shells and hardwood materials.

La Kristy Resort

This is La Kristy Resort and Cold Spring, a hidden paradise yet to be discovered. Nothing beats a dip in this pool of cold water on a hot day, or just a normal exhausting day and wanting a relaxing day off. This place is perfect for those who like adventure, a connection with mother nature and people looking for relaxation.

Tangke Nature Spring

Tangke Nature Spring Resort is located at Sitio Marios, Brgy Bacolod, Juban, Sorsogon. It is a serene spot for nature enthusiasts and families looking for a refreshing getaway.
